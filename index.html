Mikel Hernaez - Machine Learning for BioMedicine (ML4BM) group

 

Principal Investigator, Machine Learning for Biomedicine (ML4BM) Group
Director, Computational Biology Program
CIMA University of Navarra, Spain

Adjunct Professor, Department of Physics and Applied Mathematics
Adjunct Professor (by courtesy), Department of Biochemistry and genetics
University of Navarra, Spain

Our group focuses on developing and applying machine learning models, with an emphasis on (graph) representation learning, to solve open biomedical questions in the context of advance lethal diseases. We aim at taking deep learning models to the clinical practice to accelerate the path towards personalize medicine.
